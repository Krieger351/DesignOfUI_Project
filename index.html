This app would allow you to find what beers are on tap at local bars and provide deals and directions to the bars in your area. You would also be able to find certain beers in the stores in your area. The app would also allow you to talk into your phone at the end of the night and it will indicate if you are too drunk to drive.

Bar Search

  • Standard location search
  • Can use current location

Beer Search

  • Able to search by many attributes of the beer.
    • International or Domestic
    • Price
    • Location
    • Style
  • Used to find beers based on preferences.

Since there are a large amount of students in college in the U.S. who drink, there are plenty of bars that would want to opt in to this app to advertise what they have on tap. With many bars wanting to advertise, you would save considerably on your trips to the bar by finding the best deals. You would also save gas since you would not need to go all over town looking for your favorite beer.

The audience of the app will be young people over the age of 21. This demographic has a heavy population of smartphone users. This app will be able to show beer fanatics where they can find their favorite specialty beers and targets those looking for rare brews. Instead of having to enter a bar and asking the bartender what is on tap that night hoping that they have one of their favorite brews, users will be able to search for which bar has exactly what they are looking for.
